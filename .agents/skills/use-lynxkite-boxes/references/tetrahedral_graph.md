**Tetrahedral graph:**
Returns the 3-regular Platonic Tetrahedral graph.

Tetrahedral graph has 4 nodes and 6 edges. It is a
special case of the complete graph, K4, and wheel graph, W4.
It is one of the 5 platonic graphs [1]_.

returns:
