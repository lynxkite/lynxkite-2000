**Pappus graph:**
Returns the Pappus graph.

The Pappus graph is a cubic symmetric distance-regular graph with 18 nodes
and 27 edges. It is Hamiltonian and can be represented in LCF notation as
[5,7,-7,7,-7,-5]^3 [1]_.
